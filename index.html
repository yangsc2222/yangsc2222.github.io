ABOUT ME


This is Shu-chao YANG's homepage. He would like to be called Young YANG also. Shu-chao is a Ph.D. candidate at Institute of Logic and Cognition (ILC) and Department of Philosophy, Sun Yat-sen University, China.
Shu-chao’s research falls in interdisciplinary areas such as argumentation theory, rhetoric and communication, fallacy theory and philosophy of science. Now his research has been to get a better understanding of fallacy from the rhetoric perspective.

He is also keen on the popularization of science and philosophy in China.

He is a member of the The Cantonian Association of Logic.
He obtained his master's degree in philosophy of science from Hunan Normal University, China.

He obtained his Bachelor's degree of agriculture from Huazhong Agriculture University, China.
