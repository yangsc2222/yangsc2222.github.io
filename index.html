About me


This is Shu-chao Yang's homepage. Shu-chao is a Ph.D. candidate at Institute of Logic and Cognition (ILC) and Department of Philosophy, Sun Yat-sen University, China. Shu-chao’s research falls in interdisciplinary areas such as argumentation theory, rhetoric and communication, fallacy theory and philosophy of science. Now his research has been to get a better understanding of fallacy from the rhetoric perspective. He is also keen on the popularization of science and philosophy in China.
